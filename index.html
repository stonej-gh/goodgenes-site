What we do

Resale, tended like a garden

Every piece in the GoodGenes closet is chosen on merit — the make, the fabric, the cut — then verified, documented, and listed with care across the marketplaces you already love.

Chosen, one at a time

Premium denim and elevated pieces picked by hand against a written standard — never bought by the pound.

Every size, on purpose

Style doesn't stop at a size 12 — and neither do we. Finding beautiful plus-size pieces is the heart of the hunt, not an afterthought.

Documented like a specimen

Fabric content, provenance, true measurements, honest condition — recorded with a scientist's patience before anything is listed.

Our story

From plant genetics to hemlines

GoodGenes was founded by a plant biologist who spent years reading what makes living things last — then noticed the same truth in a well-made garment. Good structure endures. Good materials age with grace. The name isn't a pun; it's a thesis.
